# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Small clothing store owners and cashiers in Thailand. Cashiers use BogiePOS during active selling, where checkout speed, clear product search, and low-friction cart handling matter most. Store owners use it to understand sales, manage stock and products, configure promotions, and review sales history without needing a complex back-office system.

## Product Purpose

BogiePOS is a practical point-of-sale and store management system for small clothing shops. It helps staff sell items quickly, apply promotions, keep product and stock data current, and give owners a clear view of revenue, transactions, discounts, best sellers, and sales history. Success means daily store operations feel faster, easier to trust, and easier to review.

## Brand Personality

Fast, friendly, reliable. The interface should feel clean, practical, and approachable for daily use, with enough structure to support repeat operational work without feeling cold or heavy.

## Anti-references

Avoid cluttered legacy software, overly corporate SaaS styling, and flashy fashion e-commerce patterns. BogiePOS should not feel like a dense accounting back office, a generic enterprise dashboard, or a consumer shopping site built to advertise products rather than operate a store.

## Design Principles

- Prioritize checkout momentum: the POS flow should keep product search, cart review, discounts, and payment decisions direct and interruption-free.
- Make owner summaries scannable: dashboards and history screens should surface the few numbers that help a store owner act quickly.
- Keep operations familiar: forms, tables, dialogs, and navigation should use conventional product UI patterns so staff do not need training to understand them.
- Use friendliness through clarity: Thai copy, empty states, and error messages should be plain, specific, and helpful rather than technical or overly formal.
- Preserve trust in data: totals, stock counts, promotions, and destructive actions need clear hierarchy, confirmation, and consistent states.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA for contrast, focus visibility, and keyboard access across core workflows. Maintain reduced-motion support for any animation, avoid relying on color alone for stock or status meaning, and keep Thai labels readable at product UI sizes.
